Humans are a fairly new race, but their population has grown quickly, so they are quite common. They are quite versatile, and tend to be either sturdy or good with magic. Very rarely both.

Cyborgs are humans modified with technology. Fiberoptic nerves and nanobots help make cyborgs think as fast and heal quicker than the average human. Some of the technology was supplemented by alien knowledge, but for the most parts cybernetics are human or robot made.
